# Coded

**Build a store. Ship a product. Nothing in between.**

Coded is an e-commerce platform for people who want to sell — not manage software.
Design your storefront, list your products, and let us handle sourcing, fulfilment
and delivery end-to-end.

## What we do

- **Design** — a full visual builder for your storefront. If you can imagine it, you can ship it.
- **Sell** — commerce primitives (catalogue, checkout, payments, accounts) wired in from day one.
- **Fulfil** — branded products dispatched directly to your customer, worldwide, in 5 days.

## Where we are

Coded is headquartered in the Netherlands with operations across Europe and a fulfilment
network connecting merchants to the world.
